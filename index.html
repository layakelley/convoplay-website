Meet the Visionary

Laya Kelley

Laya is the architect of ConvoPlay. Her mission is to help women reclaim their power through the spoken word. She believes that every barrier you face in leadership and life can be dismantled by the right conversation, had at the right time, with the right level of courage.

"I created ConvoPlay because I was tired of watching brilliant women shrink themselves in rooms where they should have been steering the ship."
